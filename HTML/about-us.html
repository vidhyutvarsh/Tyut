About Tyut

Tyut Sports Wear was started back in 2013 by Vidhyut Sahas A, The team started producing shoes only in 2019. In addition to female and male, there is a children's line. Shoes of non-dull styles and rich colors immediately catch the eye.

We are located in Anna Nagar, Chennai along 1st Avenue.
Being an official Nike partner, we sell their shoes at convenient prices. We focus on team-designed collaborations that appeal to our global community of global independents, creatives, and free-thinkers. We create shoes to reflect the unbound lives we live and don't stop until we get it right.
